The plan behind this section is to help everyone that is trying to integrate Remita understand how to make that happen and to do this, we shall be utilizing the power of Postman so right there in your app, you can test and see the responses our endpoints will return with. 

Arranged stategically around this app are links to postman collections that you can use to test that particular feature you need to make or recieve payments. To use this link, please download the [Postman Chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/postman/fhbjgbiflinjbdggehcddcbncdddomop?hl=en) on your [Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) browser, click on the link and it will open up the collection in-app and right in yout browser.

If this isn't your cup of tea or you want a more integrated experience, simply copy the link and paste it in your Postman desktop app, and start leveraging the power of Remita powerful APIs for your use
